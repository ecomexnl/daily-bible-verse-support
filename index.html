Frequently Asked

How do I show the verse on my desktop?

Click the cross icon in the menu bar and choose Show Widget. The verse stays hidden until you turn it on.

How do I change the language or look?

Everything lives in the menu bar icon: pick a language, light or dark theme, font, size, opacity, and where the card sits on screen.

Which languages are included?

English (KJV), Arabic, French (Louis Segond), German (Luther), Spanish (Reina-Valera), and Dutch (HSV).

The verse is covering my work.

Turn on Click-Through from the menu so clicks pass through the card, or drag it to a corner, or simply choose Hide Widget.

Which Macs are supported?

macOS Catalina (10.15) and later, on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.
